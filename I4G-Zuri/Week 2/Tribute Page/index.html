Dr. Miles Munroe

A Great Bahamian Evangelist, an ordained Minister, avid Professor of the Kingdom of God, Author, Speaker and Leadership Consultant who founded and led the Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI) and Myles Munroe International (MMI).
